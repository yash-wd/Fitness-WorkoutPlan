# Fitness & Workout Plan -- Beginner to Hybrid Athlete

A complete, research-backed fitness documentation project built from real AI-assisted coaching conversations. This repo covers everything from **absolute beginner gym guidance** to an **elite hybrid athlete transformation system** -- including workout programming, nutrition planning, supplementation, recovery protocols, and the AI prompts used to generate it all.

> **Who is this for?** Anyone starting their fitness journey, especially beginners in India on a budget with hostel/mess food constraints. Also useful for prompt engineers interested in how to get high-quality fitness plans from AI.

## Key Topics Covered

- **Workout Splits** -- Push/Pull/Legs, Upper/Lower, Bro Split, Hybrid Athlete Split (with pros/cons comparison)
- **Exercise Library** -- Compound & isolation movements for every muscle group with machine alternatives
- **Progressive Overload** -- Week-by-week progression strategies for strength and hypertrophy
- **Nutrition** -- Macro targets, calorie calculations, meal timing, budget-friendly Indian food options
- **Budget Diet** -- South Indian hostel food optimization, affordable protein sources, no-cook meal ideas
- **Supplements** -- Creatine, whey protein, multivitamins -- what works, dosage, and what to skip
- **Recovery** -- Sleep protocols, mobility routines, deload weeks, injury prevention
- **Swimming Integration** -- How to combine daily swimming with gym training without overtraining
- **Body Recomposition** -- Fat loss + muscle gain simultaneously for beginners
- **AI Prompt Engineering** -- How to craft prompts that generate professional-grade fitness plans

## How to Use This Repo

1. **Complete beginner?** Start with the [Complete Fitness Guide](guides/Complete_Fitness_Guide.pdf) and the [6-Month Program](programs/six-month-program.html)
2. **Want a hybrid athlete physique?** Read the [Hybrid Athlete System](guides/Hybrid_Athlete_Fitness_System.pdf) and [Coach Blue Guide](guides/Coach_Blue_Fitness_Guide.pdf)
3. **Want the most advanced plan?** Open the [Elite Performance System V6](programs/elite-performance-system-v6.html) in your browser, and follow the [Study Roadmap](guides/Athletic-Recomposition-Study-Roadmap.pdf) to avoid overwhelm
4. **Want to create your own plan?** Use the prompts in the [prompts/](prompts/) folder with any AI (ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude)
